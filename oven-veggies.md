# Oven veggies

## Ingredients

- Main
  - Potatoes: 4-5
  - Carrots: 4-5
  - Zuchini: 1
  - Mushrooms: 400g
  - Tomatos: 1-2 handfuls
  - Onions: 1
  - Garlic cloves: 1
- Marinade
  - Olive oil
  - Salt
  - Pepper
  - Herb mix

## Steps

- Preheat oven to 175Â°C.
- Chop:
  - Potatos
  - Tomatos
  - Zuchini (remove watery core)
  - Mushrooms
  - Carrots
  - Onions
- Prepare marinade:
  - Chop garlic
  - Add oil
  - Add seasoning/herbal mix
- Mix everything in bowl
- Spread on sheet pan
- Crumble feta cheese on top
- Put in oven for 30 min
